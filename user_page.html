Privacy

Your privacy matters

We take your privacy seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Data Protection

All your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use SSL/TLS encryption for all data transfers and AES-256 encryption for data at rest.

Your saved tools, search history, and personal information are only accessible to you and are never sold or shared with advertisers.

Profile Visibility

Your profile is currently set to public, which means other users can see your saved tools collection and basic profile information.

You have full control over your privacy settings and can adjust them at any time from your account settings.

Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, download, or delete your personal data at any time. We believe in data transparency and giving you full control over your information.

If you choose to delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
