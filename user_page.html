Privacy

Your privacy matters

We take your privacy seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Data Protection

All your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use SSL/TLS for all data transfers and AES-256 for data at rest. Your saved tools, search history, and personal info are only accessible to you.

Profile Visibility

Your profile is currently set to public, meaning other users can see your saved tools collection and basic profile info. You have full control and can adjust these settings at any time.

Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, download, or delete your personal data at any time. We believe in data transparency and giving you full control over your information.

Account Deletion

If you choose to delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. This action is irreversible.
